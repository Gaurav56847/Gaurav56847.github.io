Experimental Design

We plan to express the enzymes of E. coli for the conversion of pyruvate to polylactate (PLA) in Idionella sakaiensis. This is expressing the same enzymes used by the iGEM Ionis Paris 2022 team in their E. coli. Thus, Ideonella sakainensis will both degrade PET and convert it into a usable and storable form. Along with Shewanella oneidensis, these two bacteria can then be used together to form a microbial fuel cell, where the fuel is PET.
