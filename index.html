Generating Electricity by Biodegrading Plastic


We are all aware that plastic waste management is a huge environmental issue. 

But how are we dealing with it? Apart from the 5Rs in plastic management?

Like many synthetic products, plastics take between decades and centuries to degrade.

But do you know that there are many microorganisms that have evolved to degrade plastics since its plastic into the environment?

These microbes can degrade them into smaller consumable substates that can potentially enter metabolic cycles of the cell.
    
We use one such bacteria in combination with another to produce electricity from plastic!
